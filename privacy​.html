Privacy Policy

App: Expivo   Effective Date: June 24, 2026   Contact: support@getexpivo.com

Who We Are

Expivo is operated by a sole individual developer based in Canada.

Information We Collect

Information you provide: Google account info (name, email, photo), receipt data (photos, amounts, vendors, categories, dates), CSV transaction files, workspace settings.

Collected automatically: App usage analytics via Firebase Analytics, authentication tokens via Firebase Authentication.

How We Use Your Information

To provide receipt storage, expense tracking, and analytics; to sync data via Google Drive (if enabled); to authenticate you via Google Sign-In; to improve the app.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Firebase Firestore on Google Cloud infrastructure. Receipt images backed up to Google Drive are stored in your own Drive account — we never store your images on our servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. Data is shared only with the third-party services above (required to operate the app) or if required by Canadian law.

Your Rights (PIPEDA)

Under Canada's PIPEDA, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information, and to withdraw consent. Contact us at support@getexpivo.com.

Data Deletion

Use "Delete Account" in the app's Profile tab, or email support@getexpivo.com.

Children's Privacy

Expivo is rated 4+ on the App Store. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental supervision.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use constitutes acceptance. Significant changes will be communicated via the app.
