This Privacy Policy explains how Expivo ("the App"), operated by Sonam Sodani ("I", "me", or "my"), collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Expivo iOS application.

By using Expivo, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

⸻

1. Information I Collect

a) Information You Provide
• Account information — your name, email address, and profile photo, obtained via Google Sign-In when you create an account.
• Financial and receipt data — vendor names, transaction amounts, dates, categories, tags, notes, and receipt images you manually enter or import into the App.
• Bank statement data — CSV files you choose to import. These are processed locally on your device and stored in your own Google Drive account.

b) Information Collected Automatically
• Usage data — anonymized app usage events (e.g. features used, import counts) collected via Firebase Analytics to help improve the App. This data does not include your financial information.
• Authentication tokens — Firebase Authentication stores secure tokens to keep you signed in. These are not shared with third parties beyond what is required for authentication.

c) Information I Do NOT Collect
• I do not collect your bank credentials or passwords.
• I do not read or process your bank statements beyond what you explicitly import.
• I do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or third-party marketers.

⸻

2. How I Use Your Information

I use the information collected to:
• Authenticate your identity and maintain your account
• Store and sync your receipt and expense data via Firebase Firestore
• Back up your receipts and files to your personal Google Drive account
• Provide expense categorization and analytics within the App
• Improve the App's functionality through anonymized usage analytics

⸻

3. Third-Party Services

Expivo uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---|---|---|
| Google Sign-In | Authentication | Google Privacy Policy |
| Google Drive API | Receipt file storage in your Drive | Google Privacy Policy |
| Firebase Auth & Firestore | Authentication & data sync | Firebase Privacy |
| Firebase Analytics | Anonymized usage analytics | Firebase Privacy |

Your receipt data stored in Google Drive is governed by your Google account's privacy settings. I do not have independent access to your Google Drive beyond what you authorize through the App.

⸻

4. Data Storage and Security

• Your expense metadata (vendor names, amounts, dates, categories) is stored in Firebase Firestore, which is secured by Firebase's infrastructure and access rules that restrict data to your account only.
• Receipt files and images are stored in your personal Google Drive, under a folder created by Expivo. You retain full ownership and control.
• I implement reasonable technical measures to protect your data, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

⸻

5. Data Retention and Deletion

• Your data is retained as long as your account is active.
• You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile section within the App. Upon deletion, your data is removed from Firebase Firestore. Files in Google Drive must be deleted manually from your Drive.
• You may also contact me at [your-email@gmail.com] to request data deletion.

⸻
